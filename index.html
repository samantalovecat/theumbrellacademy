Umbrella Academy is a tv-series adapted from a comic book made by Gerard Way.
In short, it's about a superhero family trying to save the world from ending.

The show has gotten 6 emmy-nominations in total,
and has been viewed by over 45 million Netflix-accounts.

The creator is not only known for this writing,
but is also known by being the frontman in the popular band My Chemical Romance

Original author: Gerard Way.
